NYC Dept. of Education Certified Vendor

The Queens Boro Bash is a free, all-ages community event hosted by Maxled Youth at Maloof Skatepark in Flushing Meadows. A full day of competition, community, free food, and prizes, open to everyone.

Founded by Max Suarez, Maxled Youth is a Queens-based after-school program that uses skateboarding to teach confidence, focus, resilience, character development, and personal growth. We partner with NYC public schools and specialize in working with students with special needs.

This event is a reflection of that mission: inclusive, community-first, and rooted in Queens.
